# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small and mid-size business owners in Surat and across India who find Navachitra through Instagram (@navachitraco) or word of mouth. They land on this page from a phone or laptop during the workday, deciding in a few seconds whether this agency looks like it can make *their* brand look good. Secondary: potential collaborators and early followers who want launch updates.

## Product Purpose

Navachitra is a social media and web design agency ("nava chitra" = "new image"). Its promise: a new image for every business we touch. This site is the agency's own storefront, so its design is the portfolio. Right now the surface is a pre-launch coming soon page whose single job is to capture email signups (via Web3Forms) and make visitors think "if this is their teaser, imagine what they'd do for me."

## Brand Personality

Fresh, confident, crafted. A studio that sweats details: generous whitespace, precise type, calm motion. Warm and human rather than corporate; premium without being cold. Editorial-minimal lane (Mobbin-like clarity: light surface, huge type, soft pill forms) with a distinct Navachitra warmth rather than a clone.

## Anti-references

- The previous version of this page: dark, bordered input boxes, centered generic template, "outdated and boring".
- Generic "COMING SOON" countdown templates, particle backgrounds, neon-on-black gradients.
- AI-slop tells: gradient text, glassmorphism cards, identical icon-card grids, bordered layouts wrapping everything in boxes.

## Design Principles

1. **The page is the portfolio.** Every pixel demonstrates the service being sold; if it looks templated, the business loses.
2. **Space is the luxury.** Whitespace, scale contrast, and restraint communicate craft louder than decoration.
3. **One job per screen.** Everything serves the email signup; nothing competes with it.
4. **Motion whispers.** Small, physical, ease-out entrances and micro-interactions; never showy, always respectful of reduced-motion.
5. **Warmth over chrome.** Tinted neutrals and a warm accent, not sterile grayscale.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast on all text and controls.
- Full keyboard operability for the form; visible focus states.
- `prefers-reduced-motion` honored: entrances and micro-animations collapse to simple fades or none.
- Form errors announced via `role="alert"`; success via `role="status"`.
